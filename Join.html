What is Attack On Titan?

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from humanoid creatures known as Titans.

Is the information on this website spoiler-free?

Our goal is to provide a balance between informative content and avoiding major spoilers. However, given the nature of an educational website, some plot details may be discussed in the context of analysis and discussion.
